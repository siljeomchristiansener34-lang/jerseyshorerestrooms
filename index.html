Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the nationwide service coverage of HST Porta Potty Rental?

We operate across the continental United States, serving both major metropolitan markets and rural or remote locations. Provide your event or project location when you reach out and our logistics team will confirm service availability for your area.

2. How do you ensure unit quality across different locations?

Our cleaning and inspection standards apply nationally. Every unit — regardless of where it's being delivered — goes through the same multi-stage preparation process before leaving our facility. We train our technicians consistently and hold them to the same standards coast to coast.

3. What types of units do you offer?

We carry standard single-stall porta potties, flushable portable restrooms, ADA-accessible units, portable restrooms with integrated sinks, luxury standalone units, and multi-stall luxury restroom trailers. We can build orders mixing unit types to match your event or project profile.

4. How far in advance should I book for a large event?

For large events requiring luxury trailers or multiple units, 3–6 weeks advance notice is recommended during peak season. For standard unit orders, 1–2 weeks is typically sufficient. Last-minute requests are accommodated when possible.

5. Do you offer same-day quotes?

Yes. Call or submit an inquiry during business hours and our team will provide a complete, itemized quote the same day — typically within a few hours of your initial contact.
